Ingredients

  • 1/2 kg mixed vegetables (eggplant, okra, bitter melon, string beans)
  • 1/4 kg pork, sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons shrimp paste (bagoong)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Saut√© garlic, onion, and tomatoes until soft.
  2. Add pork and cook until browned.
  3. Add shrimp paste and cook for 1 minute.
  4. Add the vegetables and cook until tender. Season with salt and pepper. Serve with rice.
