Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti noodles
  • 1/2 kg ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can tomato sauce
  • 1/4 cup tomato paste
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Grated cheese for topping

Instructions

  1. Cook the spaghetti noodles according to package instructions.
  2. Saut√© garlic and onion in a pan. Add the ground beef and cook until browned.
  3. Add the tomato sauce and paste, simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. Season with salt and pepper. Serve the sauce over the noodles and top with cheese.
