Ingredients

  • 1 kg chicken or pork
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/2 cup vinegar
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 tsp black peppercorns

Instructions

  1. Combine soy sauce, vinegar, garlic, and bay leaves in a pot.
  2. Add meat and marinate for 30 minutes.
  3. Simmer over medium heat until meat is tender.
  4. Season with black pepper and serve.
