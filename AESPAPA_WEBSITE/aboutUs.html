About Us

Welcome to Pinoy Recipe! We are a group of passionate individuals who turned our love for Filipino food into a meaningful endeavor. What started as a shared hobby during our college days has now become a platform dedicated to showcasing the best of Filipino cuisine. Back in college, we would bond over trying out recipes, hosting small food tastings, and dreaming of sharing our culture with a wider audience.

After graduating, we decided to take this passion to the next level, combining our knowledge and creativity to bring authentic Filipino flavors to everyone’s table. At Pinoy Recipe, we celebrate the rich traditions of Filipino cooking while embracing new ways to make these dishes accessible and enjoyable for all.

Our journey is deeply rooted in the values of family, community, and culture. Each recipe we share is a tribute to the dishes we grew up with, lovingly prepared by our elders, and now passed down to the next generation. We aim to connect with Filipinos worldwide and introduce our vibrant cuisine to those discovering it for the first time. With every recipe, we share not only the taste but also the stories and memories that make Filipino food truly special.

Our Mission

Our mission is to preserve and celebrate the diverse culinary heritage of the Philippines, one dish at a time. This vision began during our college days when we realized that our passion for cooking and food could become a way to share our culture with the world. Today, as graduates, we remain committed to showcasing the beauty of Filipino cuisine by highlighting traditional recipes and their cultural significance.

We believe that food is more than just sustenance—it’s a bridge that connects people, tells stories, and fosters a sense of belonging. Through Pinoy Recipe, we aim to honor the dishes that have been passed down through generations while inspiring others to appreciate the art and heart of Filipino cooking

Our mission goes beyond just sharing recipes. We aspire to ignite a love for Filipino food in both locals and global audiences, fostering an appreciation for its history, diversity, and unique flavors. Whether it’s through cherished family recipes or creative new takes on traditional dishes, we strive to make Filipino cuisine a source of pride and joy for everyone.

Let Pinoy Recipe be your guide to discovering the delicious and meaningful world of Filipino food, where every bite is a celebration of heritage, passion, and community.
