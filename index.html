Discover our Best Pinoy Dishes

Adobo is a savory, tangy, and rich Filipino dish made with chicken or pork, marinated in soy sauce, vinegar, garlic, and spices

Sinigang is a sour Filipino soup typically made with pork, tamarind, and a variety of vegetables, perfect for hot days.

Lumpia are Filipino spring rolls filled with a variety of ingredients like ground meat, vegetables, and shrimp, often served as appetizers

kare-kare is a rich Filipino stew made with oxtail, tripes, and vegetables, served with a savory peanut sauce.

Menudo is a Filipino dish made with pork, liver, potatoes, carrots, and bell peppers in a savory tomato sauce.

Sisig is a Filipino dish made with sizzling pig’s head, liver, and chili peppers, known for its crispy and savory flavor.

kaldereta is a Filipino beef stew made with tender beef, vegetables, and a tomato-based sauce, usually served on special occasions.
