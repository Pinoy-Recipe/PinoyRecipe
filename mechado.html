Ingredients

  • 1 kg beef, sliced into chunks
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 potatoes, sliced
  • 1 bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Saut√© garlic and onion in a pan. Add beef and brown on all sides.
  2. Add the tomato sauce and soy sauce. Simmer for 30 minutes.
  3. Add the potatoes and bell pepper. Continue cooking until the beef is tender.
  4. Season with salt and pepper. Serve with rice.
